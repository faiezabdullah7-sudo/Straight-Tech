  • A6 Series: Next-Gen Intelligent IMM — 900–10,000 kN, IPD 2.0 smart process, Industry 4.0 ready
  • A5-EU Series: High-end Servo — 300–7,000 kN, precision & stability, European-market grade
  • A5-N Series: High-end Servo — 65–1,125 US Tons, optimized injection unit, uniform-stress molding
  • SKIII Series: General Purpose — reliable, cost-effective, wide material compatibility
  • LS Series: Deep-cavity Product — specialized for buckets, crates, deep containers
  • U3 Series: Bakelite Special — 900–4,800 kN, integrated feeding, thermoset compounds

All-Electric Series

FF / FF-N / FF-M — Zero Hydraulic

YIZUMI's FF Series all-electric injection molding machines deliver the highest precision, cleanest operation, and maximum energy savings. Ideal for medical, electronics, and precision consumer products. The FF-M medical variant is designed specifically for cleanroom pharmaceutical manufacturing.

  • FF Series: 900–13,800 kN — full electric, seamless automation, superior precision
  • FF-N Series: 100–1,510 US Tons — enhanced quality consistency and efficiency
  • FF-M Medical: 1,600–3,800 kN — medical-grade cleanroom configuration
  • Up to 70% energy savings vs. standard hydraulic
  • Zero hydraulic oil — clean, contamination-free operation
  • Shot weight repeatability ±0.1% for ultra-precision parts

Multi-Component

CG-P / CE-P / C-NTW / D1M — 2K/3K Molding

YIZUMI's multi-component injection molding machines enable bi-color, bi-material, overmolding, and co-injection in a single cycle — eliminating secondary assembly operations and dramatically reducing cost-per-part. The CG-P and CE-P series cover 1,600–8,000 kN for demanding automotive and consumer applications.

  • CG-P Series: 1,600–8,000 kN — high-end hydraulic multi-component
  • CE-P Series: 1,500–5,500 kN — electric multi-component, quiet & energy-efficient
  • C-NTW Series: 1,600–4,800 kN — stable, customizable, vibrant multi-material
  • D1M Series: 5,000–24,000 kN — two-platen multi-component for large parts
  • Rotary table / index plate / core-pull configurations
  • Eliminates gluing, painting & secondary assembly steps

VM Series Vertical

Vertical Plastic Injection Molding — Insert Molding

The VM Series vertical injection molding machine provides precise control and stability for insert molding applications. Its vertical injection axis makes it ideal for overmolding metal inserts, connectors, wire harnesses, and other embedded components directly into plastic parts.

  • Vertical injection axis — ideal for gravity-aided insert placement
  • Direct clamping for high rigidity and precision
  • 600–3,000 kN clamping force
  • Rotary table option for simultaneous load & mold cycles
  • Reliable operation for connectors, terminals, wire harnesses
  • Compact footprint with easy operator access
